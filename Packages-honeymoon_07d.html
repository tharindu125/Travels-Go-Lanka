Honeymoon Itineraries 07 Days - Luxury Honeymoon

Spend your honeymoon exploring Sri Lanka. It is the most romantic honeymoon imaginable. The tour starts with a restful stay in Negombo. It then takes you to the cultural triangle, hill country and the southern part of Sri Lanka showcasing the real beauty of the island nation.

"The focus of this tour itinerary is to help young families discover the gems of Sri Lanka in a leisurely pace."

📜 Tour Destinations Day by Day

Day 01

✔️ Meet our driver at the Airport and transfer to Negombo

✔️ Transfer to Pinnawala (Approximately 2 hours)

✔️ Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage

✔️ Transfer to Kandy (Approximately 1.5 hours)

✔️ Overnight Accommodation at Arunya Nature Resort

Day 02

✔️ Breakfast at the hotel

✔️ Relax in the morning

✔️ Evening visit to Kandy City

✔️ Overnight Accommodation at Arunya Nature Resort

Day 03

✔️ Breakfast at the hotel

✔️ Transfer to Nuwara Eliya (Approximately 3 hours)

✔️ Visit tea factory and tea fields

✔️ Relax in the evening

✔️ Overnight Accommodation at the Heritance Tea Factory Hotel

Day 04

✔️ Breakfast at the hotel

✔️ Full day relax in the hotel

✔️ Overnight Accommodation at the Heritance Tea Factory Hotel

Day 05

✔️ Breakfast at the hotel

✔️ Leisure walk in Gregory Lake

✔️ Transfer to Bentota (Approximately 5.5 hours)

✔️ Relax by the beach

✔️ Overnight Accommodation at The Villa Bentota

Day 06

✔️ Breakfast at the hotel

✔️ Relax by the beach

✔️ Overnight Accommodation at The Villa Bentota

Day 07

✔️ Breakfast at the hotel
